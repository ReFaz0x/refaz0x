Our Research Approach

We combine technical blockchain analysis with socio-economic research to understand how Web3 technologies can address real challenges in Latin America. Our research bridges theory and practice, informing both policy frameworks and product development.

Stablecoin Architecture & Design

Comprehensive analysis of stablecoin mechanisms, collateralization models, and stability systems. We examine trade-offs between decentralization, capital efficiency, and regulatory compliance across different stablecoin designs.

Sample Work: USDC vs DAI vs $BREAD comparative analysis, $DUX BRL-pegged stablecoin design

DeFi Protocol Analysis

In-depth technical and economic analysis of DeFi protocols, examining mechanisms, dependencies, vulnerabilities, and systemic risks. We map protocol interactions and identify critical failure points.

Sample Work: Optimism Superchain DeFi ecosystem analysis, stablecoin dependency mapping

Real World Asset (RWA) Tokenization

Research on bringing traditional assets on-chain, analyzing legal structures, custody mechanisms, and market opportunities for RWA tokenization in Latin America.

Sample Work: Celo RWA Latin America research, rare earth tokenization framework

Regenerative Finance (ReFi)

Analysis of regenerative finance mechanisms, environmental impact verification systems, and sustainable funding models that align financial incentives with ecological restoration.

Sample Work: Brazilian environmental service payment mechanisms, Ekonavi sustainable asset structuring

Blockchain for Social Impact

Research on blockchain applications for humanitarian response, crisis management, and social coordination systems that empower marginalized communities.

Sample Work: Liga Colaborativa humanitarian blockchain systems, blockchain for crisis justice

Regional Blockchain Adoption Studies

Analysis of Web3 adoption patterns, regulatory frameworks, and ecosystem development strategies specific to Latin American markets and communities.

Sample Work: Celo ecosystem growth analysis, Chapada Diamantina blockchain club feasibility

Impact Assessment & Risk Frameworks

Development of assessment methodologies for evaluating Web3 projects, measuring social and environmental impact, and quantifying risks in decentralized systems.

Sample Work: XRPL Commons impact assessment tool design, stablecoin vulnerability analysis
