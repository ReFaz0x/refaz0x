Our Legal Services

We provide specialized legal advisory for the digital asset ecosystem, bridging technical blockchain infrastructure with Brazilian and Latin American regulatory frameworks.

1. Digital Asset Structuring

Tokenization and digital asset structuring in compliance with Brazilian regulation. We design legal frameworks for token issuance, asset-backed tokens, and digital securities that meet regulatory requirements while maintaining technical innovation.

2. DAO & Protocol Legal Design

Legal advisory for Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) and decentralized protocols. We structure governance frameworks, participation rules, and liability protections adapted to Latin American legal contexts.

3. Data-Proof Audit Systems

Development of audit systems with cryptographic proofs for legal processes. We design evidence collection and verification mechanisms using blockchain technology for litigation and regulatory compliance.

4. RWA Compliance

Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization and regulatory compliance. We structure legal frameworks for bringing traditional assets on-chain while maintaining full regulatory compliance in Brazilian and international jurisdictions.

5. Civil Litigation

Legal representation in civil litigation involving digital assets, smart contracts, and blockchain-related disputes. We combine traditional litigation expertise with deep technical understanding of Web3 systems.
