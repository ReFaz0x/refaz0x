Success Stories

42 Rio Partnership | 2025

From Zero to Hackathon Winners in 7 Days

In partnership with 42 Rio coding school, BnE trained 10 students with zero blockchain knowledge. Within just 7 days, these students competed at Modular Carnival hackathon and won 2nd place, securing a project with Scroll. This demonstrates our methodology's effectiveness in rapid skill development.

Chapada Diamantina | 2025

Reaching Remote Communities

Our Chapada Diamantina bootcamp attracted 250 participants and established the region's first Blockchain Club. This initiative is now expanding to 27 cities across Bahia, bringing Web3 education to underserved rural communities.

Modular Carnival | 2025

Largest Youth Web3 Mobilization

Mobilized 1,200+ registrations and 800 in-person attendees for Modular Carnival in Belo Horizonte - the largest youth turnout for any Web3 event in the city's history.
