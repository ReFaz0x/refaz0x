Active contributions across Latin America's Web3 ecosystem.

Example Projects

🌱 GreenChain Brasil

Carbon credit tracking for Amazon conservation

ReFaz0x provides technical architecture and legal framework development for a blockchain-based carbon credit system. We're helping design smart contracts that ensure transparent tracking of conservation efforts.

🏘️ FavelaDAO

Community governance for urban development

We're supporting the development of decentralized governance structures that give favela communities direct control over development funding. Our role includes creating legal structures that bridge traditional Brazilian law with DAO governance.

💧 AquaToken

Water resource management through tokenization

ReFaz0x is developing the legal and technical framework for tokenizing water rights in drought-affected regions. We're creating smart contract systems that enable transparent water allocation.

🌾 AgriConnect

Direct farmer-to-market blockchain platform

We provide blockchain infrastructure and legal advisory for connecting small farmers directly with buyers. Our work includes developing supply chain tracking smart contracts and creating cooperative governance models.

🏥 SaúdeChain

Decentralized health records for underserved communities

ReFaz0x is architecting a privacy-preserving health record system for communities with limited healthcare access. We're developing LGPD-compliant smart contracts.

📚 EduChain

Blockchain credentials for alternative education

We're building a credential verification system for non-traditional educational programs in Latin America. Our work includes developing NFT-based certification systems.
